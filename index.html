What We Do

Core Services

Comprehensive boiler engineering — from performance studies to full system re-engineering

⚙️

Boiler Performance & Operational Studies

Detailed evaluation of steam generation, fuel consumption, heat transfer, and efficiency — identifying gaps and improving output.

🛠

Shutdown & Audit Services

Pre-shutdown, shutdown, and post-shutdown audits with pressure part healthiness checks and thickness mapping at flue gas erosion zones.

🔍

Troubleshooting & Root Cause Analysis

Diagnosis of tube failures, combustion issues, LOI control, and bed coil tube failure analysis with corrective recommendations.

🚀

Capacity Enhancement & Re-Engineering

Design modifications to safely increase boiler capacity, including fluidized bed combustion re-engineering and system optimization.

🌱

Energy & Fuel Optimization

Energy conservation, alternative fuel feasibility, combustion analysis for biomass/coal blends, and fuel feeding system assessments.

💻

CFD Analysis & IBR Engineering

CFD analysis for boiler ducting, IBR-compliant piping design, stress analysis, and complete auxiliary equipment optimization.
